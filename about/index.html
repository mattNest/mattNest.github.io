Matthew Hu is a junior student at Dept. of Information Management and Finance, National Chiao Tung University and an IT Advisory/Risk Management Consulting Intern at KPMG.


Due to his background, Matthew is passionate about using tools and knowledge in Data Science to bring great insights into the financial industries.

Matthew worked as a research assistant in NCTU Fintech Center, Sep. 2017 - Jul 2018. During his time at the Fintech Center,he participated in Blockchain Research Project (Concord Securities associated with Ministry of Science and Tech.), developed convertible bond trading system prototype based on Ethereum Smart Contracts and programmed Ethereum trading contracts. He has won several awards during his college life, including International ICT Innovative Service Awards and APICTA (Taiwan Representative).

Matthew worked as a RD and quantitative trading intern at Bincentive, a decentralized asset management and mirror trading company located at Taipei 101, 43F during Jul-Sep, 2019.
