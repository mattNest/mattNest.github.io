Steve Jobs Connecting the dots

Author: Matthew Hu Date: February 14, 2019  17:20:54 Category: Speech

今天看JR Lee Radio的時候，聽到Catherine講connecting the dots這個思維覺得蠻酷的
就到youtube上面看了完整的Steve Jobs演講(點下圖連結），真的很勵志欸！
許多看起來沒什麼用的經歷零星成點，經過時間逐漸串連成一條線，最後串成綿密的網。

You cannot connect the dots looking forward. You can only connect them looking backwards. So you have to trust that the dots will somehow connect in your future. Because believing that the dots will connect down the road will give you the confidence to follow your heart, even when it leads you to the well worn path and that will make all the difference.
