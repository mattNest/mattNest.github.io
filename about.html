Autobiography

Matthew (Ming Hsiu) Hu graduated from Dept. of Information Management and Finance, National Chiao Tung University, Hsinchu, Taiwan. Due to his background, he has a solid and rigorus background in both Finance and Computer Science. He is actively seeking to study in Master of Data Science, Business Analysis and Quantitative Finance in 2021 Fall.

At the IT field, Matthew had been working as an IT Advisory in Management & Risk Consulting Internin at KPMG, Taipei. During his internship, he delivered AI Governance for Enterprise Methodology, assisting internal control for AI business solution. Furthermore, he delivered MITRE ATT&CK for ICS Risk Scenario Soultions with ISO27001 controls, which later became the prototype for KPMG to serve ICS related clients.

At the Finance field, Matthew had been working as a RD & Quantitative Trading Intern at Bincentive, Taipei. During his internship, he prepared VIP's clients' daily investment performance reports using Python and Excel, developed trading strategies management system using Python Django Framework and supported the quantitative team's trading strategies(mostly medium/low technical analysis strategies) development using C++/Python.

As for academic research, Matthew had been working as a Research Assistant in NCTU FinTech Center. He participated in two research projects. The first is the Blockchain project with Concord Securities. In that project, he developed convertible bond trading system prototype based on Ethereum Smart Contracts, wrote two trading contracts for primary market and secondary market and designed trading system's full-stack website. The second is the empirical asset pricing via machine learning / deep learning project. In that project, he constructed 1D-CNN and LSTM for holding period return prediction using Pytorch.
